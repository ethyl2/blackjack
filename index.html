Let's Play Blackjack!

×

Rules:

  1. Ace is always high (equaling 11, not 1).
  2. The game begins with both the player and the dealer receiving 2 cards.
  3. If a player's cards add up to more than 21, then she/he is bust and loses.
  4. If a player's cards add up to exactly 21, then she/he gets a blackjack and wins!
  5. If a player has been dealt 5 cards, and those cards add up to less than 21, the player wins because of a "5 card trick!"
  6. If a player's cards add up to less than 21, he/she has 2 options:
    1. He/she may get another card, which automatically causes the dealer to also get another card, or
    2. He/she may choose to stick, ending the game. When stick is chosen, if his/her cards add up to more than the dealer's cards' total, he/she wins!

Click on the deck to get another card. Click on the green hat guy to stick and end the game.
